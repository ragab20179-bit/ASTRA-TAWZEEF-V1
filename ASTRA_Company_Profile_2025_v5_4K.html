About ASTRA — Vision & Mission
SAUDI ARABIA'S FIRST FULLY AI-MANAGED STERILIZATION PACKAGING MANUFACTURER
Our Vision

To be the leading manufacturer of sterile barrier systems in the Arabian Peninsula, protecting patient safety through precision manufacturing, AI-driven quality assurance, and sustainable supply chain practices aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.

Our Mission

ASTRA manufactures ISO 11607-compliant sterilization pouches, rolls, and wraps that safeguard the sterility of medical instruments from the production line to the point of care. We serve Saudi Arabia's 500+ hospitals, 2,000+ clinics, and the broader GCC healthcare network through the NUPCO national procurement framework.

Strategic Positioning: ASTRA is the only domestic manufacturer of the full sterilization packaging product range in Saudi Arabia, replacing 100% imported supply with locally manufactured, SFDA-certified, NUPCO-listed products — directly supporting the Kingdom's healthcare localization agenda.
